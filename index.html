Collaborating Companies

We deal on both wholesale and retail sales.  Also we collaborate with different tiles producing company

Royal Ceramics

Royal Ceramics appears to be a company focused on manufacturing and supplying various types of tiles, including ceramic, porcelain, and vitrified tiles, as well as related building materials like tile gum and grout.

Goodwill Ceramics

​

Goodwill Ceramic Limited is a Nigerian company that manufactures and distributes a wide range of ceramic and porcelain tiles, with a focus on quality, reliability, and sustainability.  They are known for their high-end bathroom solutions and floor finishing products. 

Virony Ceramics

Virony ceramics, particularly Virony tiles, are known for their use in wall and floor decoration and are often made from a mix of clay and silica, with a high-temperature firing process that results in a unique texture, says Buildings and More.

Time Ceramics

Time Ceramics is a multinational group that manufactures and distributes ceramic tiles, operating in China, Nigeria, and Pakistan. They have been in business since 1995 and emphasize innovation, product quality, and customer service.

Royal Castle

Royal Castle Ceramics is a brand of wall and floor tiles, likely focusing on ceramic products. They offer a range of sizes and finishes, including SuperColor® technology for two-sided printing. Their products are advertised for use in various applications, such as homes, industries, and workspaces, according to a vendor.
